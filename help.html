Instrucions

  • Cavlier and Cabinet:
    After clicking on one of the options a text box will pop up where you can enter an integer.
    Later click anywhere inside the white box to activate the function.
  • Prespective and Parallel:
    Click on one of those buttons and then click anywhere inside the white box to activate the function, no need to enter any numeric value.
  • Rotate:
    In order to rotate the elements first click on the desired rotation (x, y ro z). A text box will pop up for entering a rotation degree.
  • Scaling:
    In order to increase the elemets' size click on the 'Scale Up' button and then click anywhere inside the white box as many times as you like.
    In order to decrease the elemets' size click on the 'Scale Down' button and then click anywhere inside the white box as many times as you like.
  • Reload:
    To reset all your actions and go bsck to the original position click on the 'Reload' button.
  • In case of invalid input the pop up will not dissapear until a valid input will be entered.
